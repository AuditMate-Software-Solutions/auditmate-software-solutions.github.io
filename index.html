About

AuditMate captures and records the state of a system at a point in time for auditing and change tracking.

  • System hostname, OS, uptime
  • Users (admins + standard users)
  • Running services
  • Installed packages
  • Open ports
  • Baseline comparison (drift detection)

Runs completely offline and outputs structured reports for inspection and debugging.
