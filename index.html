What it does

AuditMate records a snapshot of your system and lets you see exactly what changed later.

  • Users and admin accounts
  • Running services
  • Installed packages
  • Open ports
  • System drift detection

Why it exists

Most tools show dashboards or logs. AuditMate shows verifiable system state over time — offline and repeatable.
