About

AuditMate is a portable system tool that collects and records the exact state of your computer at a given moment. You can:

  • See exactly what users and administrators exist
  • Check running programs and services
  • Monitor open ports and firewall settings
  • Compare changes over time

Everything works offline, requires no installation, and produces clear, easy-to-read reports you can use for audits, debugging, or security checks.
